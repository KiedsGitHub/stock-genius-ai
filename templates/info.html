About StockGeniusAI

This is a subset of artificial intelligence (AI) that focuses on using historical data from Yahoo Finance to predict stock market movements. This Machine Learning model is trained to forecast whether stock prices will rise or fall the next day, helping determine optimal dates for buying or selling stocks.

This model is trained to predict stock prices for the following stocks:

  • Apple Inc (AAPL)
  • Microsoft (MSFT)
  • Google (GOOGL)
  • Amazon (AMZN)
  • Meta (META)
  • Starbucks (SBUX)
  • McDonald's (MCD)
  • CVS Health (CVS)
  • Visa (V)
  • MasterCard (MA)
  • Adobe (ADBE)
  • China Petroleum (600028)
